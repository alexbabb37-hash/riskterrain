How It Works

From address to report
in seconds.

No consultants. No site visits required for a first assessment. Just type an address and get an instant, data-driven risk report.

1

Enter a location

Type any Toronto store or property address. RiskTerrain geocodes it instantly and pulls all relevant crime data within the surrounding area.

2

Get your score

Receive an overall Retail Safety Score out of 100, plus sub-scores for Break & Enter, Robbery, Assault, and Auto Theft — each with a risk level and incident count.

3

Download your report

Get a professional PDF report with an executive summary, key risk drivers, site review checklist, priority CPTED strategies, and tailored recommendations.
